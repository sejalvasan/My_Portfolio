I am a 2023 IT graduate, I currently work at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer in the Automotive team. My team enables customers to shop for parts and accessories that fit their vehicles. Pretty cool, right? Previously, I have worked at Salesforce and ZS Associates, gaining diverse industry experience and enhancing my technical skills.

I focus on continuous learning, deep diving and implementing new concepts into practical applications. Whether it's mastering the latest technologies or exploring innovative solutions, I embrace challenges and approach them with enthusiasm.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find great joy in traveling and dancing. In an ideal world, I'd be coding with a view of the beach, taking in the waves while working on projects, and maybe even taking a dance break in between.
